About Us

Signora is your interactive platform for learning sign language through gamified lessons and real-world practice. Sign Language plays a vital role in making the world more inclusive, yet it’s often overlooked in mainstream education. We wanted to build something that’s not only educational but also an accessible and meaningful tool that empowers both hearing and non-hearing individuals to bridge the communication gap.
